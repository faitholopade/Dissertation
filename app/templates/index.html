About this prototype

This interface demonstrates the queryability of the FRIA knowledge graph, a semantic representation of 150 AI incident records annotated against the EU AI Act Annex III framework. The graph encodes four annotation axes: Annex III domain, fundamental rights, harm types, and system patterns.

Deployers conducting a Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment under Article 27 of the AI Act can use this interface to retrieve relevant precedent incidents based on their system's domain and risk profile.
